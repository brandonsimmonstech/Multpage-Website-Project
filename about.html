About Georgia

Georgia, situated in the southeastern region of the United States, exudes a strong Southern charm, while also evolving into a modern powerhouse, primarily propelled by its capital city, Atlanta. The state's reputation for peaches, though often associated with it, stems from anecdotes dating back to the Civil War, it is not have the world's leading peach producer nor does it have the finest peaches. Furthermore, Georgia was a pivotal location during the Civil Rights Movement as the hometown of Dr. Martin Luther King. It is also home to the Well known Georgia Aquarium, ranked as the fourth-largest aquarium globally.
