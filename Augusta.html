Augusta

Augusta, Georgia, is a charming city known for its deep-rooted connection to golf,having hosted the prestigious Masters Tournament every year at the Augusta National Golf Club. Situated along the Savannah River, the city boasts a beautiful riverfront, historic neighborhoods, and a thriving arts and cultural scene. Augusta's blend of Southern hospitality, outdoor activities, and golfing traditions make it a captivating destination for sports enthusiasts and history buffs alike.
